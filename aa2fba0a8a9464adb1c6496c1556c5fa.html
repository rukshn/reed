    Scrolling through #couplk, #lka, #srilanka timelines, selected 12 accounts incl. my own which had retweeted the video with a comment or tweeted it with original video embedded. Dozens more have just retweeted. First 2 ways give us some metrics to work with. #couplk #lka

    Google Doc https://t.co/Ts68JQsNFN features a dozen accounts, with link to tweet of video and metrics accurate at the time of data capture. Keep in mind those who see a tweet may be far more than follower count. E.g. metrics from my own tweet. #couplk #lka

    My interest this video follows from study in how #couplk has infiltrated #lka & #srilanka discourse https://t.co/Q6oPrB1Nhz. The general timbre of conversation on Twitter (beyond/outside the specific pro/anti-coup #-tags) is already critical of/oppositional to SLPP.

    Over just these dozen accounts on #Twitter (#Facebook virality tracking is fiendishly more complex for any piece of content), this one video over past 24hrs generated (at the time of capture) 491 retweets, 1,216 likes & was viewed by at least 146,738. #couplk #lka

    By way of comparison, in the pro-SLPP #Twitter cluster of 59 accounts monitored, the top 8 managed ~680 interactions in total, including NR's official account. This 1 video in a day generated more attention, over just 12 accounts, than all of SLPP's leading accounts. #couplk #lka

    What does the dispersion of this video look like over 12 accounts? Limiting data capture to 5 most recent tweets & max 100 followers/friends per user, the resulting network map reveals inter-connections & spread of content. #lka #couplk

    In dozen accounts that tweeted video are some of the most influential nodes in the #lka/#srilanka system. Video clearly anchored to 12 distinct communities (i.e. accounts) and analytics indicate diversity & diffusion, meaning video seen by many more than usual suspects. #couplk

    Note dataviz captures just the connections between dozen accounts that shared video. Each account's followers may have gone on to retweet/retweet with comment. I just can't track that, so in a sense, virality/spread likely exponentially greater than what I show here. #couplk
